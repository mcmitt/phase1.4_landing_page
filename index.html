About Us

We opened Little Things food truck in 2014 in Someplace, NJ and found immediate demand for our hot wings.

We immediately began to expand our wing sauces to fit the many different flavors our customers asked about.

At this point, we had six signature wing sauces to choose from.

In 2018 we opened our brick and mortar Little Things restaurant and created a low key atmosphere of sharing wonderful food with family and friends. At this point we doubled the number of signature sauces.

Due to the success of our take-out business we created a drive-thru, added kiosk ordering and payment to avoid the queue, and enabled online and mobile ordering with pickup at the drive-thru window.

Today we have 18 wing preparations ready for your enjoyment.
